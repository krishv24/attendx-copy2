# AI Attendance System - Features Overview & Access Guide

**Yes, the application completely fulfills all 9 of these core requirements!** All of the sophisticated AI modeling runs chronologically exactly when an Administrator clicks **"Run Analysis"** in the dashboard. 

Here is exactly how you can access and view every single one of these capabilities within the application:

### 1. Uses AI to study past attendance records
* **How it works:** The *Data Ingestion Agent* automatically pulls 30-day simulated attendance footprints from the MySQL database and translates strict mathematical models into contextual JSON structures for the AI to read.
* **How to access it:** 
  1. Log in as an Admin (`admin@school.com`).
  2. Click **Students** in the top navigation bar.
  3. You will mathematically see the exact historical percentages the AI pulled for every student.

### 2. Identifies students who are at risk of falling below required attendance
* **How it works:** The *Risk Scoring Agent* groups students into strictly defined AI matrices mapping to `Low`, `Medium`, `High`, or `Critical`.
* **How to access it:**
  1. From the **Admin Dashboard**, look at the main KPI cards to see total numerical breakdowns of Risk Distributions.
  2. Click on the **Students** tab. You'll see beautiful color-coded badges (`Critical` in red, `Low` in green) next to each individual student.
  
### 3. Detects irregular patterns like frequent absences, subject avoidance, or declining engagement
* **How it works:** The *Pattern Detection Agent* batches stringified histories together and runs Google Gemini to actively sniff out qualitative habits (e.g. "Skipping every other Monday"). 
* **How to access it:**
  1. Log in as an Admin and naturally click on **Alerts**. 
  2. Look for alerts flagged visually with the `Pattern` or `Anomaly` identifier. It translates raw data into human-readable text warnings.

### 4. Predicts future attendance percentages based on past data
* **How it works:** The *Prediction Agent* runs a Sci-Kit Learn strict Linear Regression over consecutive integer day arrays to forecast 30 days into the future.
* **How to access it:**
  1. You can see the explicit "Predicted Rate" directly on the **Admin Dashboard** active students table mapping future statistics locally.

### 5. Sends early alerts before attendance drops too low
* **How it works:** The *Alert Agent* proactively fires permanent records explicitly linking a student's declining DB footprint to an actionable warning if their AI prediction drops below 75%.
* **How to access it:**
  1. Admin view: Click **Alerts** on the navbar to see a system-wide log of triggered warnings.
  2. Student view: Have a student log into their own portal and click **My Alerts** to see the exact warnings isolated just to their account.

### 6. Suggests personalized actions like mentoring, counseling, or extra academic support
* **How it works:** The *Recommendation Agent* builds a brief contextual prompt merging the student's name, current mathematical prediction, and exact risk level, requesting Gemini for 1-sentence personalized academic support strategy.
* **How to access it:**
  1. Have a mock 'High Risk' student log in (e.g., `bob@school.com` with password `student123`).
  2. Have them navigate to **Recommendations**. They will see warm, human-like AI text advising them on specific academic interventions.

### 7. Flags unusual cases such as sudden mass absences or abnormal attendance spikes
* **How it works:** The *Anomaly Detection Agent* processes daily total class absence rates up against a maximum mathematical cap (20%). It tells Gemini to explicitly flag sweeping institutional absences across dates.
* **How to access it:**
  1. Log in as an Admin.
  2. Check the **Reports** or **Alerts** tab. Mass anomaly tags will appear referencing exact dates.

### 8. Helps administrators make data-driven decisions
* **How it works:** The *Reporting Agent* compiles 150-word Executive Summaries mapping massive JSON models over to narrative text dynamically.
* **How to access it:**
  1. Log in as an Admin.
  2. Navigate to **Reports**. You will see the chronological "Comprehensive AI Attendance Report" available for reading.

### 9. Improves overall student engagement and academic performance
* **How it works:** By providing a beautiful, transparent student-facing portal, it motivates failing students to see a path to recovery dynamically. 
* **How to access it:**
  1. Log out of Admin and explore the `student123` authenticated dashboard! You'll see clean graphs, read alerts privately, and consume AI recommendations tailored exclusively to student success arrays.
